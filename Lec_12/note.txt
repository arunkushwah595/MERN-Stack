<.> Advantages of React.
     1. It has a concept of vIRTUAL DOM.
     2. Component Based Architecture.  (Components are pieces of code)
        There are following types of Components.
            a. Functional Based Components. (used for static change like variable change array change)
            b. Class Based Components. (used for dynamic change like).
     3. No Reloading. (Client side Rendering (CSR) hoti hai)
     4. JSX extension is used
     5. faster because of no reloadning.


<.> Disadvantages of React.
    1. Poor Search engine Optimization. (because it is CSR).
    2. Unidirectional flow of Data. (Parent se Child pe data bhej sakte hai but Child se Parent pe nahi)
